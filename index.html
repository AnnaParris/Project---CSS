Why join Sentry?

Sentry, although a new (fictional) company has protected many households in the Puget Sound area through preliminary testing in our hometown. The testing allowed us to advertise and gain a customer base of a few thousand, our goal is to protect homes around the world and to ensure the safety and security of your home, work, school, and even your car!

All Sentry security devices will be accessible through your moblie device, laptop, and home computer. This accessiblity will be limited to you and whoever you choose to give access.


What is Sentry?

Sentry devices are used in the desired space to secure and moniter the spaces in your home you wish to be kept safe. The device will move around to ensure that you are receiving a full view of your home, workplace, school, or car, this is fully optional of course, but choosing to disable this feature will diminish the effectiveness of the sentry security devices.
